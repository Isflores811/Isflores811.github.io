About Me

Welcome to my creative portfolio. My journey began in early 2022 with a clear purpose: to demonstrate to myself that hard work directed toward a goal can be enjoyable and deeply rewarding, an aspect of life that has been missing until I started working on indulging on my creative side. To get there I made a choice to replace my off time with a commitment to learning and self-improvement. Starting with web development, I embarked on this path, fueled by a determination to acquire new skills from the ground up. The journey had also led me to branch into new places, compelling me to dive into graphic design, photography, filmmaking, and various other methods for expressing my creative ideas. Here's to many years of dedicated effort in honing and mastering my craft!
